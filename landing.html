Total_CPI

Total Consumer Price Index (CPI) isa measure that examines the weighted average of prices of a basket of consumer goods and services, such as transportation, food, and medical care. It is calculated by taking price changes for each item in the predetermined basket of goods and averaging them.

Source
CPI_(less_energy)

The "Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers: All Items Less Food & Energy" isan aggregate of prices paid by urban consumers for a typical basket of goods, excluding food and energy. This measurement, known as "Core CPI," is widely used by economists because food and energy have very volatile prices.
